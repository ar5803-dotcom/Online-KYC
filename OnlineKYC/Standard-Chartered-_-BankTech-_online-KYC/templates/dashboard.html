KYC Verification Process

The purpose of KYC (Know Your Customer) Verification is establishing the identity of any customer. In online KYC, the identity of the person is verified electronically via PAN-BASED (or any other ID) substantiation. Thus KYC provides proof of identity of the person to the service provider instantly, dispensing the need for the tedious in-person verification.




Complete your E-KYC Verification in 3 Simple Steps :

STEP 1 : Login & click the "Start Process" button on your Dashboard

STEP 2 : Upload your PAN CARD

STEP 3 : Click "Open Camera" displayed on the screen and look into the camera till the process the completed

Your Verification process is completed and the result will be displayed on the screen

STEP 4 : Add your PAN-CARD Number and your Full Name as per the PAN-CARD (Uploaded Government Id) for OCR Code Verification

Your OCR Code Verification process is completed and the result will be displayed on the screen
